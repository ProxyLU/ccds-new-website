March 13, 2017

CCDS Alumnus Meets Officers of T.I.n.Y. Berkeley Chinese Drama and Entertainment Company

On Mar. 13, 2017, CCDS Alumnus and Co-founder Qingyun Xie (谢清昀) met with several officers of T.I.n.Y. Berkeley Chinese Drama and Entertainment Company, a student-run theatre group based in University of California, Berkeley, during his visit. Both sides shared the experiences of both drama groups, discussed a range of issues and explored ways to enhance interactions.

This meeting highlights the efforts of the CCDS and CCDS Alumni Association to expand our network of friends for the benefit of CCDS and the Chinese collegiate theatre scene at large.

May 6, 2017

Visit to Yangtze Repertory Theatre of America

On May 6, 2017, a CCDS delegation led by President-elect, Haoxing Pu (蒲昊星), attended the performance Twin Restaurants (双馆计), staged in New York, NY by Yangtze Repertory Theatre of America. Founded in 1992, Yangtze Repertory Theatre of America, Inc. (美国长江剧团) is a professional Asian theatre company based in New York, NY.

After the performance, the delegation attended a question-and-answer session with the artistic team, cast and crew. The CCDS delegation called on Executive Director of Yangtze Repertory Theatre, Mr. Wayne Chang (张明伟), Artistic Director of Yangtze Repertory Theatre, Mr. K.K. Wong (黄锦江), and Director of performance, Mr. Tony Wang (王皓松). They discussed plans for further interaction between the two theatre groups.

Group photograph of CCDS delegation, with the artistic team and cast.
CCDS members include: Haoxing Pu, Xiaoning Wang (王晓宁) , Zhiyao Wang (王芷瑶), Danyang Han (韩丹阳), Yong Zhang (张雍), and Yutong Wu (吴雨桐) (from left, 8th to 13th).

This is our second visit to the Yangtze Repertory Theatre, following the inaugural visit in November 2016. We are grateful for the support of Yangtze Repertory Theatre to the CCDS cause.

April 22, 2017

Visit by Princeton Chinese Theatre

On April 22, 2017, President, Princeton Chinese Theatre, Letian Wang (王乐天), led a delegation to visit CCDS, at the invitation of President, CCDS, Yilin Liu (刘宜霖). Princeton Chinese Theatre (普林斯顿中文剧社) is a student-run theatre group based in Princeton University, Princeton, NJ.

During the visit, the PCT delegation met with President, CCDS, Yilin Liu; Vice-President, Haoxing Pu (蒲昊星); Director of Production, Xiaoning Wang (王晓宁). They had in-depth exchanges on the recent development of both drama groups.

This visit reaffirms the warm ties between CCDS and PCT. We hope to enhance our interactions in the next academic year for the benefit of both groups and our members.
