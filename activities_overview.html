Acting and Technical Workshops

CCDS organizes a series of acting workshops for our members, with the efforts culminating in a workshop production scheduled for April 22, 2018. In addition, our long-established technical workshops introduce the basics of theatre and lighting to cast and crew.

Click here for more information on the workshop production.

Exchange Visits

We organize regular exchanges with other Chinese drama groups across U.S. universities. Visits by theatre professionals and scholars are also planned whenver such opportunities are available. Stay updated on our mailing list.

Read more about our wide network of partner organizations here.

General Meetings

General Meetings, held twice every semester, will be announced over the mailing list. Click here for more information.

Gatherings and Retreats

The CCDS is a closely knit community of drama enthusiasts. At the end of every semester, we organize gatherings in conjunction with the cast party of the production. In addition, retreats are held to promote friendship and bonding among our members. All members are welcome to join in.
