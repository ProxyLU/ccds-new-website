Distinguished Visitor Program

CCDS Distinguished Visitor Program was launched in 2017, with the mission of promoting cultural exchange and widening the exposure for CCDS members in performing arts. Through a series of interactive workshops and guest performances, our Distinguished Visitors bridge the link between a collegiate theatre group like CCDS and the professional theatre arts scene.

Since its founding, CCDS has been at the forefront of exchange and outreach with the broader performing community – collegiate theatre groups and performing arts professionals alike. Having recognized the utmost importance of multi-faceted interactions with the community, we are proud to be the first collegiate drama group to conduct structured programs for our distinguished visitors. Our frequent interactions with fellow collegiate theatre groups, theatre professionals and scholars provide us with expertise, advice, resources that are indispensable to our activities as an independent student organization.

Our inaugural speaker was Professor Claire Conceison, prominent American scholar of contemporary Chinese theatre at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and longtime advocate for Sino-US cultural exchange. During her visit in Fall 2017, CCDS members and the Cornell community had extensive interactions with her over multiple events, and benefitted from her profound insights and experience. The event attracted attention from various backgrounds, as well as several academic departments of the university.

Looking ahead at Fall 2018, we hope to invite a theatre practitioner to share multicultural experiences in the performing arts scene, in order to offer the Cornell community a chance to explore theatre as an exciting career in a multicultural context. This program will serve as a complement to the insights of scholarly research and critiques which were presented in last year's program.

We are always encouraged by performing arts professionals and collegiate theatre enthusiasts who desire to deepen their engagement with CCDS. More information on our external relations is available here.
