CCDS Alumni Association

Cornell Chinese Drama Society Alumni Association (Chinese: 境缘剧社校友会) is a informal network of alumni of the CCDS.

CCDS Alumni Association became operational on Feb. 19, 2016. An inauguration ceremony was held on Apr. 16, 2016. (Read the report here.)

Objectives

The objectives of the Alumni Association are as follows:

  • To serve as a network for the ex-members of Cornell Chinese Drama Society (hereinafter referred to as “Society”) who have graduated from Cornell University, to bond, to interact and to sustain friendships;
  • To encourage alumni to compassionately give back to the Society;
  • To pool the resources offered by the Society alumni for the benefit of the Society, current members of the Society and its alumni.
Activities

As an informal network, the CCDS Alumni Association seeks to maintain bonds among alumni, and to connect alumni and current members. The CCDS Alumni Association facilitates the connection between the alumni and the CCDS through regular visits and conference calls.
